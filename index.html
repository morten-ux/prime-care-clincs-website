Who We Are

The Organisation Behind the Brands

Prime Care Clinics operates as a holding and management group, providing the infrastructure, governance, and strategic direction that enables each brand to thrive independently while benefiting from shared expertise.

We do not operate patient-facing services directly. Our role is to build and support the organisations that do.

Brand Development

We identify, acquire, and develop cosmetic clinic brands with strong clinical foundations and growth potential.

Operational Support

Centralised HR, compliance, procurement, and technology services free our brands to focus on patient care.

Clinical Governance

A shared governance framework ensures safety, quality, and regulatory compliance across every brand we operate.

Strategic Growth

We support expansion through capital allocation, market analysis, and multi-site operational expertise.
